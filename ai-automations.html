How It Works

Every engagement follows the same three phases.

No one-off builds that break in a month. Every automation we build follows a disciplined process from audit to hand-off to ongoing maintenance.

Phase 01
Assessment & Build

We audit your business for automation opportunities, prioritize by time saved, and build the system. You see exactly what we're building and why before we start.

Phase 02
Hand-off

We train you and your team on how it works, what to watch, and when to step in. Everything is documented. You're never dependent on us to keep it running.

Phase 03
Maintenance

Monthly check-ins to keep it running, catch edge cases, and evolve as your business changes. You're not on your own once we build it.
