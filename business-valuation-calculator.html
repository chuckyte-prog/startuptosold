SBA Bankability ?Most small businesses are purchased using an SBA loan. The bank checks whether the business earns enough to cover the loan payments after paying a manager to replace the owner. This is called the Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR). Banks require at least 1.25x, meaning the business earns 25% more than the loan payments. If it doesn't qualify, most buyers can't get funding to buy your business.
