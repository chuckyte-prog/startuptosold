How It Works

Three phases. Marketing that runs itself.

I build your content system, set up the automation, and handle the ongoing optimization -- so your marketing stays consistent whether you're slammed or slow.

Phase 01
Content Strategy

I map your audience, define the content pillars that match your services, and build a 90-day content calendar. AI drafts the posts; you approve the brand voice before anything goes live.

Phase 02
Build & Launch

I connect your social channels, email platform, and ad accounts, set up the posting schedules, and launch the first campaigns. You approve, I execute.

Phase 03
Optimize

Monthly performance review -- what's getting engagement, what's driving leads, what to adjust. I update the content calendar and ad targeting based on what's working.

What I Build

Every automation in this category.

These are the specific marketing automations I build. Most clients start with the social posting calendar and email newsletter -- visible, consistent, and zero weekly effort once live.

AI Content Calendar
AI drafts social posts, captions, and email subject lines based on your services, season, and recent jobs. You review a month of content in one sitting and approve. I schedule and post automatically.
Email Newsletter Automation
A monthly or bi-weekly email to your customer list -- tips, seasonal reminders, promotions. AI drafts, you approve. Sent automatically on schedule to keep you top-of-mind without manual effort.
Paid Ad Optimization
AI monitors your Google or Meta ad performance, flags underperforming campaigns, and recommends budget shifts. You get a weekly summary; I handle the adjustments based on your approval.
Seasonal Campaign Builder
Pre-planned campaigns for your peak seasons -- spring tune-up pushes, holiday gift card promotions, back-to-school service reminders. Built once, triggered automatically each year.
