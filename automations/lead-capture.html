What We Build

Every automation in this category.

These are the specific automations we build for lead capture and follow-up. Not every client needs all of them -- the assessment tells us which ones move the needle for your business.

60-Second Auto-Response
When a web form submits, the lead gets an SMS and email within 60 seconds -- personalized, not generic. Includes a link to book. Works while you're on a job, after hours, or on weekends.
Follow-Up Sequences for Non-Bookers
A 3-5 touch cadence over 2 weeks for leads that respond but don't book. Mix of SMS and email. Stops automatically when they book or ask to stop.
Missed Call Text-Back
When you miss a call, the caller gets an automatic text: "Sorry we missed you -- here's a link to book." Simple, fast, recovers revenue you'd otherwise lose.
Lead Routing by Service Type or Geography
Assign new leads to the right team member automatically based on what service they're requesting or where they're located. No more manual triage.
AI Lead Qualification & Scoring
AI scores each incoming lead against your ideal customer profile. High-score leads get routed to CRM or pinged to your phone via Slack. Low-score leads get filtered or queued separately. You stop wasting time on leads that will never convert.
Lead Generation Pipeline
For outbound: takes a list of company domains, enriches with contact info (Apollo, Clay, Hunter.io), AI scores each against your ICP, and writes top leads to your CRM or a Google Sheet automatically. Runs on a schedule.
Personalized Cold Outreach
Lead enters from Airtable, AI pulls their LinkedIn posts and company website, Claude writes a personalized opener, and the email goes out via Instantly or Smartlead. Replies get classified automatically (interested / not now / unsubscribe), and interested leads create a task in your CRM. Requires email domain warming as part of the engagement.
