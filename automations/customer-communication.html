How It Works

Three phases. Customers who never feel forgotten.

I audit your current customer touchpoints, build the right communication sequences for your business, and keep them running and relevant month after month.

Phase 01
Assessment & Build

I map your customer journey -- from first contact to job close to re-engagement -- and identify where communication breaks down. Then I build the sequences to fill those gaps.

Phase 02
Hand-off

I train you on what triggers each message, how to update content, and how to handle the exceptions when a customer responds and needs a human touch.

Phase 03
Maintenance

Monthly check-ins to review open rates, response rates, and re-engagement results. I update messaging seasonally and as your services change.

What I Build

Every automation in this category.

These are the specific customer communication automations I build. Most clients start with the FAQ responder and job status updates -- both are high-visibility, low-effort wins.

AI FAQ Responder
Handles common inbound questions automatically -- hours, pricing, service area, booking links. Works over website chat, SMS, or Facebook Messenger. Includes a graceful hand-off to a human when the AI doesn't know the answer.
Automated Job Status Updates
When a job status changes in your field software (Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro), the customer gets a text automatically -- "Your technician is on the way," "Your job is complete," etc. No manual send, no customer calling to check in.
Post-Job Satisfaction Check-In
A short satisfaction check-in goes out automatically after job completion. Happy customers get nudged toward a review. Unhappy customers get a direct response path before they go to Google to complain.
Re-Engagement Campaigns for Past Customers
Seasonal reminders, annual service prompts, and win-back campaigns for customers who haven't returned in 6-12 months. Your best new customer is usually an old one you haven't talked to lately.
