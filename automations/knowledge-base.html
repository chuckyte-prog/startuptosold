How It Works

Three phases. A team that runs itself.

I extract what's in your head, structure it into searchable SOPs, and layer an AI assistant on top so your team can get answers in seconds -- not after interrupting you.

Phase 01
Knowledge Extraction

I interview you and your team leads to identify the questions that come up constantly. Everything that lives in someone's head gets written down, structured, and organized.

Phase 02
Build & Deploy

I structure your SOPs in a searchable knowledge base, set up an AI assistant trained on your content, and build the habit loops so your team uses it instead of asking you.

Phase 03
Keep It Current

SOPs that go stale are worthless. I build a lightweight review cadence and show you how to update content as your processes change. Monthly check-ins keep everything accurate.

What I Build

Every automation in this category.

These are the specific knowledge base and SOP automations I build. Most clients start with the SOP library and AI search assistant -- the combination eliminates the most interruptions fastest.

SOP Library Build-Out
I interview your team and document every repeatable process -- from opening procedures to how to handle a complaint call. Written, structured, and organized in a searchable system your team can actually use.
AI Search Assistant
I layer an AI assistant on top of your knowledge base so employees can ask questions in plain English and get the right SOP or policy instantly -- no digging through folders, no interrupting you.
New Hire Onboarding System
A structured onboarding sequence that walks new hires through your processes in the right order, with checkpoints, quizzes, and a clear path to being fully productive -- without requiring a week of your time.
SOP Maintenance Workflow
A recurring process that flags outdated SOPs, assigns owners, and tracks updates. Keeps your knowledge base from becoming stale without requiring a dedicated person to manage it.

Questions

What to know before we talk.

I use structured interviews with you and your team leads, combined with watching how work actually gets done -- not how you think it gets done. Most businesses are surprised by how many undocumented processes exist. The extraction phase typically takes 2-3 sessions of about 90 minutes each.
I use tools you already have or that fit your budget -- Notion, Google Workspace, or Confluence are common. I don't lock you into a proprietary system. The AI assistant sits on top of whatever you choose.
That's exactly why I build a maintenance workflow into the system. Every SOP has an owner and a review date. When a process changes, the SOP update is a 10-minute task -- not a project. A stale knowledge base is worse than no knowledge base.
Every engagement starts with an Assessment to map your processes and prioritize what to document first. The full knowledge base build is scoped after that based on complexity and how many processes need to be captured.
