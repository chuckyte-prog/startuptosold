The Pipeline

What This System Does

One command kicks off a sequence of connected operations. Each step feeds the next. By the time it finishes, the day is ready to go.

Archive & Build
Yesterday's work moves to archive. Today's plan builds itself from five calendars, with unfinished tasks carried forward automatically.
Email Cleanup
Noise gets trashed. Errors get summarized by type and count, then trashed. Only the signal makes it through.
Day-Specific Ops
Monday: spam reports processed, apology drafts created. Friday: weekly events email auto-drafted. Each day has its own routine.
GTD Triage
Voice captures from throughout the day (recorded on iPhone via Siri) get pulled from email, parsed, and routed into the right project with next actions defined. No manual sorting.
Exercise Scheduling
Gym classes checked, calendar conflicts detected, bookings made, recovery blocks added. Two weeks out, fully automated.
The Standout
Voice Capture to Structured Task

Throughout the day, I capture tasks by talking to my phone. "Remind me to follow up with the printer vendor about the invoice." "Add updating the help center to the DailyPB project." The next morning, the system pulls every capture from email, figures out which project it belongs to, and files it with a defined next action. No inbox processing. No manual sorting. Just talk, and it gets handled.

The Real Point
