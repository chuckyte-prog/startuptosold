The Pipeline

What This System Does

One command kicks off a sequence of connected operations. Each step feeds the next. By the time it finishes, the day is ready to go.

Plan the Day
Yesterday's work gets archived. Today's plan builds itself from every calendar you run, with unfinished tasks carried forward automatically.
Tame the Inbox
Noise gets cleared. Important patterns get summarized. Only the signal makes it through to the person.
Weekly Routines
Each day has its own recurring work. Weekly cleanups, recurring reports, standing drafts. The system handles them on the right day, every time.
Route Whatever Came In
Ideas, tasks, and reminders captured throughout the previous day get sorted and filed automatically. Nothing sits unassigned.
Personal Scheduling
On request, the system handles standing routines that would otherwise take time every week. Workouts, reports, standing reminders, whatever keeps falling off your list.

If it's simple, the system just does it.

Throughout the day, I capture tasks by talking to my phone. The next morning, each one either lands on the day's task list, or if it's simple enough, a browser agent runs it. No inbox processing. No manual sorting. Just talk, and it gets handled.

The Real Point

All of that can be one command, custom-built for whatever your daily operations look like. Not a generic app. Not a SaaS tool with features you don't need. A system designed around exactly how your team actually works.
